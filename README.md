# Urban Bike Share Performance Analytics Platform

## Overview

The **Urban Bike Share Performance Analytics Platform** is a business intelligence project that demonstrates how interactive analytics can support operational and strategic decision-making within a large-scale bike-sharing network.

In this project, I developed a collection of Tableau dashboards that transform historical trip data into actionable insights for transportation planners, operations teams, and business leaders. Rather than reviewing raw trip records, stakeholders can explore rider behavior, bicycle utilization, station performance, and travel trends through an intuitive, interactive reporting experience.

The dashboards help answer questions such as:

- How long are bicycles typically checked out?
- When does demand peak throughout the day?
- Which stations experience the highest traffic?
- How do customer groups differ in riding behavior?
- Which areas may require bicycle redistribution?
- What operational trends should planners monitor over time?

By consolidating multiple analyses into a centralized analytics platform, this project demonstrates how business intelligence tools can improve visibility into urban transportation systems and support data-driven decision making.

---

## Business Problem

Managing a city-wide bike-sharing system requires continuous monitoring of rider demand, bicycle availability, and station activity. Thousands of trips occur every day, making it difficult for operations teams to identify trends by reviewing raw transactional data alone.

Without interactive reporting, organizations face challenges such as:

- Uneven bicycle distribution across stations
- Increased customer wait times
- Underutilized bicycles
- Difficulty identifying demand patterns
- Limited visibility into customer usage behavior
- Reactive rather than proactive operational planning

This project addresses these challenges by providing an interactive analytics platform that converts large transportation datasets into meaningful business insights.
